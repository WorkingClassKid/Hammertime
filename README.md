## A building mod for those who actually want to… build

Hammertime is a mod designed to enrich the building experience in Project Zomboid. Originally created for my multiplayer server, it adds a wide variety of new craftable items and structures, allowing players to progress in construction without having to tear apart every house in the county.

The goal is simple: fill the lack of diversity in the vanilla content and finally offer real options to build, furnish, and customize your base.

__You can subscribe to this mod on Steam at the following address:__     
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3657204498

## What this mod offers
Hundreds of buildable structures and dozens of craftable items, including:

- __Appliances__ : BBQ, Freezers, Fridges, Stoves...
- __Crafting__ : Curtains
- __Furnitures__ : Chairs, Beds, Cabinets, Dressers, Tables....
- __Storage__ : Bookshelfs, Clothing Racks, Containers, Shelves.....
- __Structures__ : Walls, Windows, Floors, Fences, Garage Doors.....
- __Survival__ : Generators, Water Pumps, Fireplaces.....

## Vanilla‑friendly - No external dependencies

No external dependencies, no weird mechanics — Hammertime stays deliberately vanilla‑friendly to ensure maximum compatibility and long‑term updates.

Hammertime is split into several sub‑mods that you can enable as you wish, depending on what you want to add to your game.

## Compatibility

Hammertime is __not compatible with other construction mods__, since at some point they modify the same functions and files, which causes conflicts.

However, Hammertime should be compatible with all other mods that are not related to construction.

## Submit a bug report

Upload your __complete console.txt file__, along with any other relevant information, and add a description of your issue on our page as a new issue.

### Where can I find my console.txt file?

Windows: %userprofile%\Zomboid\console.txt

Mac/Linux: $User\Zomboid\console.txt
